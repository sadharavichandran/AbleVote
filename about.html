ℹ️ Who We Are

AbleVote is a mission-driven initiative dedicated to making democracy accessible to everyone. We believe that voting is not just a right, but a powerful way to create change — and no one should be left out because of barriers.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower people with disabilities by providing tools, resources, and guidance that make voting easy, accessible, and inclusive.

👀 Our Vision

A world where every citizen, regardless of ability, can fully and confidently participate in elections.

💡 Our Values

💙 Inclusion

Everyone deserves equal access.

💪 Empowerment

Supporting independence in the voting process.

🔍 Transparency

Clear and trustworthy information.

📢 Advocacy

Driving positive change in election accessibility.
